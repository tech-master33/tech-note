Welcome to TechNote App Store

What is this?

This is the App Store for TechNote. It hosts free games and apps that you can download directly into TechNote. Browse the catalog below or use the App Store inside TechNote to install them.

Why am I seeing this?

You are seeing this page because you visited the App Store in your browser. To install apps, open TechNote and press Enter on App Store from the main menu.
