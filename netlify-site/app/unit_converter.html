Unit Converter

Apps Version 1.1 • Utilities

About

Convert between common measurement units across 6 categories. Supports length, weight, temperature, speed, volume, and data storage. Instant results as you type.

Preview

Length: Meters to Feet Value: 100 Result: 328.084 100 Meters = 328.084 Feet

Supported Categories

  • Length — Meters, km, cm, mm, miles, yards, feet, inches
  • Weight — kg, g, mg, pounds, ounces, tons
  • Temperature — Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin
  • Speed — km/h, mph, m/s, knots
  • Volume — Liters, ml, gallons, quarts, cups, fl oz
  • Data — Bytes, KB, MB, GB, TB

Controls

  • Space — Next item
  • Backspace — Previous item
  • Enter — Select
  • Escape — Exit
