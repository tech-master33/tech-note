Blackjack

Games Version 1.0 • Card Game

About

Beat the dealer to 21 without going over. Start with $100 and place your bets. Hit to draw cards, stand to hold your hand, or double down for a chance at bigger wins. Face cards are worth 10, Aces are 1 or 11.

Preview

Balance: $120 Bet: $20 Dealer (17): K♠ 7♥ You (18): A♠ 7♦ > Hit Stand Double

Controls

  • Up / Down — Navigate menu
  • Left / Right — Adjust bet amount
  • Enter — Select action
  • Escape — Exit

Actions

  • Hit — Draw another card
  • Stand — Keep your current hand
  • Double — Double your bet, draw one card, then stand

Features

  • Full Blackjack rules with Ace handling
  • $100 starting balance
  • Adjustable bets ($5 increments)
  • Dealer follows house rules (stands on 17)
  • Voice announces all cards and totals

How to Install

Open TechNote → App Store → Blackjack → Press Enter to install.
