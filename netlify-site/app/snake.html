Snake

Games Version 1.0 • Arcade Classic

About

Guide the snake to eat food and grow longer. The snake moves automatically — just change direction to avoid walls and yourself. Each food eaten makes the snake longer and the game faster. How long can you survive?

Controls

  • Arrow keys — Change direction
  • P — Pause / Resume
  • Escape — Exit

Features

  • Auto-advancing game loop
  • 30x15 play field
  • Speed increases as you grow
  • High score tracking
  • Pause support
  • Voice announces food eaten and game over

Open TechNote → App Store → Snake → Press Enter to install.
