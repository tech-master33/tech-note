Hangman

Games Version 1.1 • Word Guessing Game

About

Guess the hidden word one letter at a time before the hangman is complete. You get 6 wrong guesses before the game is over. Words are chosen from a bank of 40+ words covering technology, nature, and everyday objects.

Preview

Word: _ _ _ _ _ _ _ Wrong: 2/6 Guessed: A, E, S, T, R Remaining: 4 chances

Controls

  • Space — Next option
  • Backspace — Previous option
  • Enter — Select
  • N — New game
  • Escape — Exit game

Features

  • 40+ word bank
  • Tracks wins and losses
  • Shows guessed letters
  • Voice feedback for each guess

How to Install

Open TechNote → App Store → Hangman → Press Enter to install.
