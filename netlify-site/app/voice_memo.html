Voice Memos

Apps Version 1.0 • Record Audio

About

Record, play back, and manage voice memos. Recordings are saved as WAV files in your TechNote voice memos folder. Perfect for quick voice notes and reminders.

Preview

Voice Memos > memo_2025_01_15.wav (32s) memo_2025_01_14.wav (45s) memo_2025_01_13.wav (18s) Recording... 0:00:32

Controls

  • Space — Next memo / Start recording
  • Backspace — Previous memo
  • Enter — Play / Stop recording
  • Delete — Delete memo
  • Escape — Exit

Features

  • Record voice memos as WAV files
  • Play back recordings with full voice
  • Delete unwanted memos
  • Recordings saved to ~/.tech-soft/voice_memos/

How to Install

Open TechNote → App Store → Voice Memos → Press Enter to install.
